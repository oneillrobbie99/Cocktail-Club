Espresso Martini

  • • 40ml of Vodka
  • • 20ml of Coffee Liqueur
  • • 20ml of Espresso
  • • Coffee Beans for Garnish
  • • Ice

Fill a shaker with the ice, vodka, coffee liqueur and espresso.

Shake well for one minute until the shaker is ice cold.

Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with coffee beans.

Strawberry Cosmopolitan

  • • 90ml of Strawberry Infused Vodka
  • • 15ml of Cointreau
  • • 15ml of Lime Juice
  • • 2 Strawberries for Garnish

Muddle one strawberry in a shaker until all juices are released.

Add the ice, vodka, cointreau and lime juice.

Shake for 30 seconds.

Strain into a cocktail glass and garnish with the remaining strawberry.
